Privacy Policy for NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury

Effective Date: January 2026

NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury is an offline sliding puzzle game inspired by real walks and locations across the Canterbury region of New Zealand. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Data Collection

NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury does not collect, transmit, or share personal or sensitive user data.

Data Storage

All game progress, unlocked artwork, and user preferences are stored locally on the user’s device only. No data is uploaded to external servers or cloud services.

Sharing Content

The app includes an optional sharing feature that allows users to share unlocked artwork and the app name using other applications installed on their device. This action is performed entirely on the user’s device. NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury does not track, log, or store any shared content.

Accounts and Authentication

NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury does not require account creation, login, or user authentication.

Notifications

Optional local notifications may be used to enhance the gameplay experience. Notification data is processed locally on the device and is not transmitted externally.

User Control

Users have full control over their data and may reset game progress or remove the app at any time using standard device controls.

In-App Purchases

NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock additional content. Payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The app does not collect, store, or have access to payment or financial information.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play services strictly for in-app billing and essential app distribution functions. These services may process limited technical information as required by Google Play. NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury does not access, collect, or store this information.

Children’s Privacy

NZ Sliding Puzzles: Canterbury is intended for a general audience. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. As the app functions offline and without user accounts, no personal data is collected from any users, including children.
